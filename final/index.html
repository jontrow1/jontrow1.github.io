Overnight Rafting

By Jon Trowbridge

Come and join us for a two day rafting trip. We spend ony a few hours going over safety, prepping the rafts and the itinerary. We will then spend the majority of the day rafting down the river. We will then set up camp at a designated location here we will sleep under the stars and get a feel of what it means to camp in Idaho.

The next morning we will get up, pack the camp up again and then head back out on the raft. We will spend another full day enjoying the rapids and views that the river has to offer. Don't worry about how far we have traveled, a bus will meet us at the end of our journey to take us back to the "office" for a fantastic dinner to celebrate our wonderful journey.
