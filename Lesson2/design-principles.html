Design Principles for Web Developing

Hick's Law

Hillsboro County Public Library

Hillsboro County Library chooses to minimize choices by condensing them down to only a few choices. From the choices they give, the user will then be directed to another page where choices are once again categorized/filtered for ease of use.

Fitt's Law

Zillow

Zillow exemplifies Fitt's Law, where the very first and typically only thing you noticed above the fold is the search bar.

White Space and Clean Design

DnD Beyond

DnD Beyond uses their whitespace to grab attention to their content. With a dark background, the whitespace is part of the content they place upon their site.
