Target Audience

The target audience is anyone living within those areas who want to check on the weather for their activities outdoors. It's also for those who are planning activities indoors who want to make sure that it's potentially going to be yuck outside.

Persona 1: Jamie Hall

Occupation: Accountant
Demographics and Education: 35 yr old, single male, with a CPA from DeVry University
Goals and Motivations: One day he will have enough money to start a family but until that day comes, being proud of his work will suffice.
Environment: He works for a company of nearly 100 people and he has gotten to know over 70% of them. Utilizing different programs at work, he has been able to find shortcuts around most issues.
Quote: "It's tee-shirt weather outside."

Persona 2: Teri Webber

Occupation: Stay-at-home mom
Demographics and Education: 25 yr old, married female, mother of two, with a bachelor's degree in Marriage and Family Studies from BYU-I
Goals and Motivations: She makes sure that her family has everything they need to function each day and wants her children to be well rounded with education and activities.
Environment: There isn't anything this mom can't find on her phone or computer. If a question is asked by her inquisitive children, she will be able to find it through a browser or one of her many social media groups.
Quote: "Today was a lazy day but I kept the kids alive."

Scenarios:

  1. Tomorrow is my day off and I think I would like to go mountain biking, what will the weather be like?
  2. Will it be sunny tomorrow so I can take the kids to the park to play?
  3. What coat will I need to wear to work tomorrow?
  4. Is it a jeans or shorts kind of day outside?
  5. Am I going to need an umbrella when I go watch my sons soccer game?
