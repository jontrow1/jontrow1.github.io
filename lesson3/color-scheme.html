Color Scheme

The color scheme for this site with be reminiscent of the outdoors. The colors chosen were more earthy tones, keeping the information from becoming saturated in color. The color choices below may not all be used for the color scheme but the plan is to use all of them. The background colro will be color 1/color 4. Text colors will be white/black and link colors will be color 3/color 5. Color 2 will be used for miscellaneous purposes.

Color 1: #6C96A1 | Color 2: #B1C7CC | Color 3: #ADD6E0 | Color 4: #87694D | Color 5: #E0C6AD
