Local Legend

By Jon Trowbridge

A rock with magical properties? Never! At least that is what I thought. This unassuming rock has been in the local legends for many a decades, it is said that this rock can predict the weather based on the appearance of exterior color. Only those who have studied the rock can discern its subtle variation of color to know whether it's going to rain or be clear skies. If it's true, then I am out of a job.
